## What This Video Demonstrates

Luke Pierce lays out a complete AI agency sales system — from positioning ("operational waste elimination") through a structured discovery call framework, five ROI calculation formulas, and objection handling scripts — giving agency owners a repeatable process for selling AI automation to SMBs.

## Workflow Steps

1. Position as "operational waste eliminator" — never lead with "AI automation" or "chatbots"
2. Research the prospect's industry vertical before the call; identify common time-drain patterns
3. Run discovery call: ask about team size, biggest time drains, current tools, and what they'd do with 10 extra hours/week
4. Quantify waste during the call: hours per week × hourly rate × 52 = annual cost of the problem
5. Select one of the five ROI pillars that maps to the discovered pain (lead gen, support deflection, data entry, scheduling, or reporting)
6. Present proposal: current cost vs. AI solution cost vs. annual ROI — show the math explicitly
7. Handle objections with prepared scripts ("we already use software" → show the gaps; "too expensive" → return to ROI math)
8. Close on a pilot scope, not the full vision

## Key Insight

**Position AI as "operational waste elimination" not "AI automation" — frame every proposal around hours saved × hourly rate** to make the purchase decision feel financially obvious rather than speculative.

## Reusable Primitives

- Operational waste framing (5 pillars: lead gen, support deflection, data entry, scheduling, reporting)
- Annual ROI formula: (hours saved/week × hourly rate × 52) − tool cost = annual ROI
- Discovery call question sequence (team size → time drains → current tools → 10-hour hypothetical)
- Objection handling scripts for the three most common objections
- Pilot scope close (reduce risk perception, increase yes rate)
- Proposal format: current cost / AI cost / net ROI / payback period

## Limitations / Gotchas

- ROI formulas require honest time estimates from prospects — sandbagging is common
- GoHighLevel + n8n stack has a meaningful learning curve for new agency owners
- The video covers the sales process well but doesn't address how to generate discovery call appointments in the first place
- Retell AI per-minute costs must be factored into deliverable pricing or margins erode
- "Operational waste" framing works for SMBs; enterprise buyers may need different language
